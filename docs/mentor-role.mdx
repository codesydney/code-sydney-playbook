# What is a mentor and how do I become one?

## Details
- Mentors at Code.Sydney help guide our other team members
- We proudly display our mentors on our [Code.Sydney website](https://www.code.sydney/)
- To become a mentor, you must have successfully delivered one project as a team lead. Get in touch with Engramar via Slack to express your interest. 

## FAQs
- Is there a skill level / industry experience level required to become a mentor?
  - No, you need to just successfully deliver a project as a team lead
